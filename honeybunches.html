Honey Bunches of Oats

Honey Bunches of Oats: Family Game Night is a rebrand of the original Honey Bunches of Oats cereal. This edition features the same classic cereal with a interactive box. The project focuses on how to utlilze the cereal box as a board game intended for young families to play together.

Research

The reasearch for this project consisted of diving into the Honey Bunches of Oats brand history and identity. Establishing the strategy of the current brand was essential to mantaining cohesion within the new edition branding. The brand centers on family, fun, and inclusivity. The vibrant colors and consistency of the box designs reflect these ideals. Below are boxes that shaped the final edition, ranging from 1989 to today.

Design Elements

The elements that make up the new edition are pulled from the existing brand. The colors adhere to the original warm yellows and browns, and the typefaces chosen reflect a new, casual feel for this edition.

Final Edition

The final edition consists of a redesigned dieline and character stickers for within the box. The color palette draws on the theme of nighttime, while keeping the original brand colors. The central logo on the front acts as a spinner, complete with tasks to win the game.
