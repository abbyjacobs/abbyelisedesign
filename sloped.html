Sloped

Sloped is a typeface inspired by the ski slopes of Breckenridge, Colorado. A city of recreation, play, and nature, Breckenridge is defined by vintage charm and modern fun. Sloped mimics these qualities through its sans serif identity and exaggerated end points.

Research and Sketches

The project began with researching the history of Breckenridge as a mining town turned recreational spot. The history, downtown signs, and the surrounding nature inspired a classic feel that incorporated more playful serifs. Initial sketches experimented with best ways to incorporate curvature and playfulness while still maintaining a classic serif look.

Final Products

The final typeface was transformed into a poster and a booklet. The poster continues to mimick the movement of the mountain slopes with cascading letters, and the booklet illustrates the story of the typeface.
