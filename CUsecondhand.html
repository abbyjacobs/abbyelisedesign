CUsecondhand

CUsecondhand is a mobile app designed to connect Champaign-Urbana college students to local secondhand stores. This awareness of local secondhand stores, as well as convenience to donate unwanted items to these stores, boosts business for shop owners and encourages thrifting locally.

Research and Sketches

CUsecondhand is a response to the lack of engagement from CU students with local thrift stores. Afer taking a research survey, results indicated that college students mostly buy clothing new, rather than buy secondhand, and they indicated that they buy from non-local thrift stores rather than local stores.
