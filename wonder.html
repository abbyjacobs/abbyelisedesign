Wonder

Wonder is a brand identity for a prospective outdoor museum exhibition. Based off of a randomly selected city and theme, Wonder takes place in Dallas, TX and focuses on light via astromonical bodies and the great expanse of space. The prospective event partners with Perot Museum of Nature and Science to bring the indoor museum exhbition outdoors for families and adults to enjoy.

Identity System

Before any other design element can be created, a strong identity system is established to give life to the brand. The event itself attracts families with young children, so the identity system follows suit. Child-like curiosity inspired the bright colors, logo figure, and playful illustration style.

Poster

The Wonder Poster invokes a sense of human smallness against the vastness of the universe. This feeling of smallness - and wonder - is achieved through the sizing of the figure and the focus on the planets. The information of the event is placed in the corners to not distract from the primary illustration.

Merch

The merch for Wonder was designed with the primary target audiences in mind: children and their parents. The nightlight incorporates childhoood fears of the dark into a space-themed solution, and the tote bag and signage facilitate parents with their event experience.

Social Media Campaign

The final design elements include a social media capmaign to encourage attendance and raise awarenesss of the event. The posts are designed for Instagram with a heavy empahsis on the visual elements and minimal text to reach parents of young children.
